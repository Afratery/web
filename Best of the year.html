Mikumi National Park

Mikumi National Park is a stunning wildlife reserve in Tanzania, covering 3,230 square kilometers. It's known for its vast open plains, scenic mountains, and abundant wildlife, including elephants, giraffes, lions, leopards, buffaloes, zebras, wildebeests and over 400 bird species. The park's landscape is similar to the Serengeti, with acacia trees and baobabs dotting the savannah. Mikumi is easily accessible from Dar es Salaam, making it a popular destination for day trips or longer safaris

Mount Kilimanjaro

Mount Kilimanjaro is the highest mountain in Africa and the highest freestanding mountain in the world, standing at 5,895 meters (19,341 feet) above sea level. Located in Tanzania, it's a dormant volcano with three distinct volcanic cones: Kibo, Mawenzi, and Shira. The mountain is known for its diverse ecosystems, ranging from tropical rainforests to alpine deserts and arctic summits. Climbers can choose from several routes to the summit, Uhuru Peak, which is a challenging but rewarding experience due to the breathtaking views and unique wildlife. The name "Kilimanjaro" means "mountain of whiteness" or "'shining mountain," likely due to its snow-capped peak

Tarangire National Park

Tarangire National Park is a renowned wildlife reserve in northern Tanzania, known for its vast elephant population and iconic baobab trees. Covering an area of 2,850 square kilometers, the park's diverse landscape features granite ridges, river valleys, swamps and acacia woodlands. The Tarangire River, which flows through the park, is a lifeline for animals during the dry season, attracting large herds of elephants, wildebeests, zebras and other wildlife.

Arusha National Park

Arusha National Park is a compact yet diverse wildlife reserve in northern Tanzania, covering 552 square kilometers. It's known for its stunning scenery, with Momella Lakes, Ngurdoto Crater, and Mount Meru, Tanzania's second-highest peak. The park is home to a wide range of wildlife, including giraffes, elephants, buffaloes, monkeys, and over 400 bird species.
